Brief:
	This project is to establish a small online book store. It will follow Amazon style as much as possible. As the limitation of assignments, it will only include JQuery for Javascript and no PHP framework such as Laveral will be implemented in this project. The layout and style will all depended on CSS3 design.

Appearance:
	Due to limited techniques, simple and less animated pages will be expected.

Functionality:
	1) All Users:
				- View products (name, description, reviews)
				- Search products (simple and complex search)
				- Cart record in cookies

	2) Register Users:
				- Include all functionality of common users
				- Account management (login name, email, phone...)
				- Purchase management (cart, order, invoice, payment, shipping...)
				- Store previous view/plan/order information on database
				- Comment on product reviews

	4) Administrator:
				- Add/Adjust/Remove products (price, description, sales, category ...)
				- Sent register user message/email
				- Generate financial report
				- Generate orders and invoice

	3) Simple search:
				- by category
				- by author
				- by book name

	4) Complex search:
				- by other features like publish year...
				- by two or three features together
				- result sort by price, alphabetical, etc

				primary key: productid
				foreign key:

	3) order table
				primary key: orderid
				foreign key: userid

				foreign key: userid, productid

	5) cart table
				primary key: cartid
